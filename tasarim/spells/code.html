Ritual Steps

I

Prepare the Space

Begin by clearing your mind. Light your sage bundle and gently wave the smoke around your body and your sacred space to cleanse any lingering negative energy.

II

Ignite the Flame

Place the white candle in front of you. As you light it, visualize a bright, protective light surrounding you. State your intention for release out loud.

III

The Release

On your piece of paper, write down everything you wish to let go of. Fears, doubts, old habits. When finished, safely burn the paper in a fireproof bowl using the candle's flame.

IV

Closing

Sit in silence as the paper turns to ash. Thank the moon for her guidance. Extinguish the candle to close the circle.
