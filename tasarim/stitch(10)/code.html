Celestial Rituals

Full Moon Release

Harness the powerful energy of the full moon to release what no longer serves you. This ritual cleanses the spirit and opens the path for new beginnings.

Ingredients

The Ritual

I

Cleanse Your Space

Begin by lighting the sage. Waft the smoke into the corners of your room, visualizing the heavy, stagnant energy dissipating into the air. Chant softly: "I clear this space of all that is not love."

II

Light the Candle

Sit comfortably before your bowl of water. Light the white candle, focusing on its steady flame. This flame represents the light of your intuition guiding you through the darkness.

III

Release & Let Go

Dip your fingers into the water. As you touch the cool surface, imagine your worries flowing out from your fingertips and being neutralized by the water. Speak aloud what you wish to release.

IV

Close the Circle

Blow out the candle to signify the end of the ritual. Pour the water outside into the earth to ground the energy.

"As I will it, so mote it be."
