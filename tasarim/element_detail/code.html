The Black Candle is a powerful tool in any witch's arsenal. Often misunderstood, it is not used for malevolence but for absorption of negativity. It acts as a spiritual vacuum, clearing stagnant energy, banishing bad habits, and creating boundaries of steel.

auto_awesome Ritual Usage

    Cleanse & Anoint

    Cleanse the candle with smoke. Carve your intention into the wax. Anoint with protection oil from top to bottom (away from you) to banish.

  • 2
    Set the Alter

    Place on a fire-safe dish. Surround with salt or tourmaline crystals for amplification.

  • 3
    Ignite & Focus

    Light the flame. Visualize the black wax absorbing the negativity in your life as it melts away. Let it burn completely.
